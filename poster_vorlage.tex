% ── Helper: placeholder box ───────────────────────────────────
% Usage: \placeholder[width]{height}{label}
\newcommand{\placeholder}[3][\linewidth]{%
  \fbox{%
    \parbox[c][#2][c]{#1}{%
      \centering\color{gray}\small #3%
    }%
  }%
}

% ── Section heading (green bold + rule) ───────────────────────

  \vspace{4mm}%
  {\color{HohenheimGreen}\bfseries\large #1}\\[-1mm]%
  {\color{HohenheimGreen}\rule{\linewidth}{1.5pt}}%
  \vspace{2mm}%
}

  \postersubsec{Resistenztests:}

  Describe laboratory resistance tests. Young plants are raised under
  controlled conditions, artificially inoculated and examined for disease

  expanded leaves to quantify pathogen presence and understand colonisation
  behaviour.

  \postersection{3.~~Ergebnisse}

  Summarise key field results in 1–2 sentences. State whether complete

  \vspace{3mm}

  e.g.\ endophytic behaviour in early infection stages (Abb.~3 B, right).
